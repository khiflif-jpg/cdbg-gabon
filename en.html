CDBG is based in Bitam, in Northern Gabon

Located in northern Gabon, Bitam is the heart of our forestry operations.
This biodiversity-rich region allows us to develop sustainable exploitation
on 10,000 hectares of private property.

About us

Expertise dedicated to Gabon's forest

10,000 hectares

of privately-owned land sustainably managed in Bitam, northern Gabon.

Tropical woods

Selection and trade of noble species: Okoumé, Azobé, Kevazingo, Padouk, Ebony, Moabi, Tali, Iroko, Movingui, Bilinga...

Local employment

Development of a sawing activity with local labor to strengthen Bitam's economy.

Our ambition

Master the entire value chain of Gabon's wood, from forest to international export.

Our services

A complete value chain

Logging

Techniques respectful of the environment and biodiversity.

Sawing

Primary transformation with modern equipment and skilled labor.

Logistics

Transport and logistics adapted to national market requirements.

Our commitments

Environmental respect

Our project puts ecology, wildlife and flora at the heart of every action.

Socio-economic development

Creating local jobs and supporting the socio-economic development of the population in Bitam region are at the core of our commitments.

Value chain mastery

Our ambition is to control the entire wood value chain from the forest to international export.
